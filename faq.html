FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you have questions? We have the answers! We have compiled the most frequently asked questions by you, so we would have the answers for you right away. Please take your time to read it below but should you have any additional questions, just send us a message and we will get back to you shortly!

Q: What is Business Globe Awards?

Business Globe Awards was created with the vision to ensure that small to mid-sized businesses with limited to no marketing budgets are able to get the industry recognition they deserve for providing truly exceptional service and excellence in consumer forward practices.

BGA strives to provide value for both businesses and consumerswhileemphasizing on the positive actions of companies of all types in today’s digital world conduct.

Our panelists include 5 highly accomplished and successful individuals that understand what it takes to build a successful, long-term and customer-oriented business and are experts in their respective fields.

Q: Who can nominate a company

A: Anybody can submit a nomination for an evaluation by BGA.

Q: Can the business nominate itself?

A: Yes, we take self-nominations as well.

Q: How does this entire process work?

A: STEP 1: Upon receiving the nomination, BGA contacts the nominated business based on the contact information we receive from the person submitted the nomination.

STEP 2:Once we establish contact, we ask the business if they wish to be considered to be evaluated by BGA. There is no obligation to be evaluated by BGA and the participation for all businesses are completely voluntary.

STEP 3: Should the business decide to go through with it, the evaluation process begins. Our Tier 1 support gathers publicly accessible information on the business and prepares an initial assessment that is entered into our database.

STEP 3: Our panelists gather (virtually) together to vote.

STEP 4: At a previously established date, the winners are announced on the website and we also send out an email notification once the decision has been made (whether it is favorable to your business or not).

Q: How long does this entire process take?

A: From the moment you submit your initial evaluation inquiry till we publish the winners on our website, it can take a few weeks winner announcements). Rest assured, you will be properly notified about the outcome of our panelists’ decision before we reveal our list of winners on our website.

Q: How much does this entire process cost?

A: FREE! While other websites charge for the nomination, evaluation or potential promotion on their websites, it is our pleasure to provide this service to small to mid-sized companies that need the exposure absolutely complimentary.

Q: Why do you even offer a FREE service? What are you trying to accomplish?

A: We believe in promoting small to mid-size businesses that have no or barely any digital exposure. Nowadays, negative feedback spreads very quickly but the positive news is “barely news”. This is a direction we intend to change by promoting smaller companies for their excellence and the positive impact they make in their customers’ lives.

Q: What entity types qualify for an evaluation?

A: Any kind of business qualifies with ONE exception. At this time, we cannot accept evaluation requests for sole proprietorships or any foreign equivalents of that.

Q: Why aren’t you showing the names of the five panelists? What are you afraid of?

A: The reason why we don’t display the names of our panelists is simply that it is proven to be the best way to keep this process legitimate and to avoid any type of direct solicitation. By not knowing the exact panelist, no business will have the ability to meet any of them in person and try to use illegal or unethical ways to influences our panelists’ decisions (aka. freebies, discounts on services, trading favors). We have a zero-tolerance policy against that, period.
