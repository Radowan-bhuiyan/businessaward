MEET OUR PANELISTS:

A lot of the success of our independent awards process here at BGA comes down to the fact that we have a diverse panel of five individuals from around the world – five individuals with different nationalities, ages, life experiences, educational backgrounds, and careers – that allows us to provide a much more independent, inclusive and diverse award evaluation process than anyone else in the business.

We recognize that businesses today are looking to take advantage of their excellence and to be recognized as industry leaders in their respective areas. We also appreciate how much of an impact having different viewpoints can have on the awards evaluation process, particularly when the opinions of our independent panelists come from such disparate viewpoints.

In an effort to maintain the prestige of BGA Awards, businesses receiving awards from our program must gain at least three positive votes out of the five individual panelists we have on board. Our panelists contain the following 5 individuals:

CEO
Travel Industry

Our first panelist is from Eastern-Europe, who has been in the travel industry since the late ’90s. A hard-working and dedicated person whose emphasis is on customer satisfaction and constant innovation.

PANELIST TIP: Get this panelist’s vote by demonstrating top-notch customer service and show us how you are part of your industry’s change.

Investor
Travel Industry

Our second panelist is based in the USA with over 40 years of experience in the tech industry (when most people did not know much about it) who is by now an investor in multi-million-dollar tech companies.

PANELIST TIP: Get this panelist’s vote by demonstrating cutting edge technological background and by showing how these tools enable you to provide your customers with an easier way to conduct business with your company.

Personal Chef
Gastronomy Industry

Our second panelist is based in the USA with over 40 years of experience in the tech industry (when most people did not know much about it) who is by now an investor in multi-million-dollar tech companies.

PANELIST TIP: Get this panelist’s vote by inspiring your industry competitors and your clients with a unique vision and perfection (or near that) in execution while showing us your “secret sauce” that puts you above the rest in your field.

Retired Attorney
Legal Field

Our fourth panelist is based in the USA, graduated summa cum laude from one of the top law schools with a law-enforcement background (working for the sheriff’s department) who became a licensed attorney and worked at top corporations as a chief attorney. Retired in 2012 from active work, we are truly delighted to have him on board.

PANELIST TIP: Get this panelist’s vote by demonstrating ethical and professional con duct for your business and staying in compliance with your Federal, State,and local municipality licensing requirements.

Small Business Owner
Entertainment Industry

Our final panelist is originally from Latin-America but based in the USA; a fashionista millennial,just so that we can have a completely different set of eyes and point of view. Eyes for attention to details, whatever the other four of the panelists may have missed, this panelist will surely find it..

PANELIST TIP: Get this panelist’s vote by showing what makes you trendy and the envy of your competitors. Show us all the buzz you created around your brand and you will succeed.
